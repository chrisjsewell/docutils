Release 0.1 (2002-04-??)
========================

This is the first release of Docutils, merged from the now inactive
reStructuredText__ and `Docstring Processing System`__ projects.  For
the pre-Docutils history, see the `reStructuredText HISTORY.txt`__ and
the `DPS HISTORY.txt`__ files.

__ http://structuredtext.sourceforge.net/
__ http://docstring.sourceforge.net/
__ http://structuredtext.sourceforge.net/HISTORY.txt
__ http://docstring.sourceforge.net/HISTORY.txt
