===============================================
Latex Writer Variants
===============================================

:Author: Guenter Milde <milde@users.berlios.de>
:Date: $Date$


This sandbox project is a place to try out and discuss variants of the
latex2e writer.

The aim is twofold:

* enhancements or improvements of the "standard" latex writer

* development of custom latex writers for special needs. Some
  are in there own sandbox ``dkuhlman/Docs``, ``docpy-writer``,
  ``jensj/latexmath``, ``latex_directive`` and  ``verse-writer``.
