What is a PSoC?
================

.. r2b-simplecolumns::
	:width: 0.95

	- Micro-controller
        - programmable in C or assembly
	- 24 MHz
	- 32 kB Flash ROM
	- 2 kB SRAM
	- connection pins
	- Cost: < $10 (chip)
	- $130 for the eval board w/chip

	.. image:: PSoC_06_cropped
		:width: 1.85in
		:align: center


Why micro-controllers?
=======================


- cheap
- small
- put them in anything
- $12 billion/year industry
