What is roff
============

roff, in it's many variants nroff, troff, groff, are UNIX programms for
formatting text files.

* nroff is for line printers (who ever can remeber these) and output on
  screen, man pages (?) .
* troff is the typesetting variant.
* groff is the gnu version.

Why roff
========

Fun, another way to make rst to postscript. roff is impressive small
and quick.
